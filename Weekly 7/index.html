DEEPFAKES AND CHEAP FAKES

by The Data & Society report

The advent of synthetic media like deepfakes and cheap fakes has complicated the digital information. The ability to create convincingly realistic manipulated videos creates significant challenge to information verification and media trust. Anyone with access to the technology with a set of training data can create a deepfake.

However, in the broader context of misinformation, the introduction of deepfakes might be seen as a continuation of long-standing practices rather than a completely new phenomenon. Propaganda, disinformation, and manipulation have been common throughout history; what deepfakes do is provide another, more advanced tool to carry out these practices.

The realistic deepfakes creates technical difficulties of detection.
