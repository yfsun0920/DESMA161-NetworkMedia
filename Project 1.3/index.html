Tao Te Ching
道德经

The Tao that can be told of

Is not the Absolute Tao;

道可道，非常道

The Names that can be given

Are not Absolute Names.

名可名，非常名

The Nameless is the origin of

无名天地之始

Heaven and Earth;

有名万物之母

The Named is the Mother of

All Things.

Therefore: Oftentimes,

One strips oneself of passion

故常无欲，以观其妙；

In order to see the Secret of Life;

One regards life with passion,

常有欲，以观其徼

In order to see its manifest forms.

These two are the same

此两者同出而异名

They are given different names

When they become manifest.

They may both be called

同谓之玄

the Cosmic Mystery:

Reaching from the Mystery

into the Deeper Mystery

玄之又玄

Is the Gate to the Secret of All Life.

众妙之门
