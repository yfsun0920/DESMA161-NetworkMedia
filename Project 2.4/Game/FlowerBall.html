Flower Ball is a game about making a gift for the little bunny by rolling the ball and collecting flowers. The game sets in a small forest where the bunny lives. The player controls the rolling ball with WASD. keys to find out all the flowers in the map. The ladybugs are waling ememies which will eat the ball and restart the level.
