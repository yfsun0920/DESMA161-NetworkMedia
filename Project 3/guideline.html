UBI Guidelines

Welcome to UBIVERSE, where we aim to redefine financial freedom for every individual through Universal Basic Income (UBI). To ensure clarity and maintain harmony within our community, we've set up the following UBI guidelines:

Eligibility

Every registered member of UBIVERSE is eligible to receive UBI. You only need to sign up and confirm your account to start receiving daily UBI.

UBI Collection

Each day, a 'Collect UBI' button will be activated on your account. To receive your UBI, simply click this button within 24 hours. Remember to collect daily, as uncollected UBI does not accumulate.

Usage

The UBI you receive is real money, and it's yours to spend however you like. Pay bills, invest, donate, or use it for leisure - the choice is yours.

Accountability

The UBI you receive is for your personal use. Misuse, such as creating multiple accounts to receive additional UBI or selling your account, is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account suspension.

Sustainability

Our AI-driven system generates income to distribute as UBI. This self-sustaining approach ensures consistency and fairness in UBI distribution.

Support

Our dedicated support team is always ready to assist with any issues or queries. Feel free to contact us for help with your UBI.
